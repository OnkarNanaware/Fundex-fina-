Who Fundex Serves

Built for Every Stakeholder - Fundex was born from the desire to restore trust — between donors and impact, between vision and execution, between promise and proof.

For Donors Who Demand Proof —
Fundex gives you complete visibility into every funding request, expensef receipt, and fraud flag. See exactly where your money goes, with AI-generated summaries and GST-validated proof delivered in real time.
For NGOs Who Value Integrity —
Admins approve volunteer requests with one click. Volunteers upload receipts and proof images instantly. Our AI extracts vendor, date, and amount — then flags mismatches, duplicates, and overspending so you stay clean and compliant.
What Powers Fundex

Fundex combines human accountability with AI precision — ensuring every expense request, receipt upload, and verification step is tracked, validated, and visible to those who fund the mission.

The Workflow

Volunteers request funds. Admins approve or reject. Receipts are uploaded with proof images. AI extracts data. Admins verify. Donors see the complete cycle — from purpose to proof, every step documented and fraud-checked.

The AI Layer

Google Vision OCR reads receipts. Rule-based fraud detection flags amount mismatches, duplicate uploads, and overspending. GST validation ensures tax compliance. AI summaries turn raw data into donor-friendly impact stories — automatically.

The Vision

Our vision is accountability without bureaucracy. Fundex protects what matters — donor trust, NGO reputation, and volunteer dignity. We invite organizations ready to lead with transparency and funders who believe every rupee deserves a receipt.
